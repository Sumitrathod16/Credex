## Outreach Script (use this to find interview subjects)

**Twitter/X DM:**
> "Hey [name] — I saw your post about [AI tool / tech costs]. I'm building a free AI spend audit tool and looking for 10 minutes with someone who manages a dev team's tooling budget. No pitch, just want to learn. Happy to share early access as a thank-you. Worth a quick call?"

**IndieHackers / Slack group post:**
> "Looking to talk to 3 founders/CTOs for 10 min about AI tool costs — specifically how you track and review what you're spending on Cursor, Claude, etc. Building something and want real feedback before I ship. Comment here or DM me."

**Interview questions to ask:**
1. Walk me through how you decided which AI tools your team uses today.
2. Do you know what you're paying in total across all AI tools each month, off the top of your head?
3. Have you ever compared your AI spend to alternatives or thought about optimizing it?
4. If you found out you were $500/mo over where you needed to be, what would you do with that information?
5. What would make you trust a tool that told you to switch from your current AI stack?
6. If someone sent you a link to this kind of tool, what would make you click vs ignore it?
